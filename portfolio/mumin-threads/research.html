🌙 Mumin Threads - Comprehensive Startup Research

📋 Executive Summary

This document outlines a lean startup approach for Mumin Threads with a maximum budget of $2,000 for initial setup, focusing on testing the market before scaling.

1️⃣ Tirupur Suppliers Overview

Why Tirupur? Known as the "Knitwear capital of India" - 90%+ of India's exported hosiery products, low manufacturing costs, bulk production capability, experienced in kids clothing.

Key Supplier Directories:

  • IndiaMART: dir.indiamart.com/tiruppur/kids-wear.html (500+ suppliers)
  • ExportersIndia: www.exportersindia.com/tirupur/kids-clothing.htm
  • Go4WorldBusiness: Bulk wholesale platform

Recommended Suppliers to Contact:

  1. Bigben India (bigbenindia.in) - Boys clothing specialists
  2. Rex Apparels (rexapparels.com) - Kids wear manufacturer, exports
  3. Kalyan Textiles - Cotton knits, kids wear
  4. Sree Jothi Knits - OEM/ODM for Islamic kids wear
  5. Sri Selvam Creations - Private label manufacturing

MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity):

  • Typically: 100-500 pieces per design/color
  • Custom labels: 500-1000 pieces
  • Sample orders: 20-50 pieces possible

✅ Key Takeaways

  1. Start lean - $2000 is enough to test
  2. Break-even at 12-15 units/month - Very achievable
  3. Focus on 3 things first: Product quality, Instagram presence, Customer service
  4. Reinvest profits - Don't take salary until Month 6
  5. Expect losses Month 1-2 - This is normal
  6. Key success factor: Build trust with Muslim community
