🛡️ HOW TO AVOID ANY LOSS

Rule 1: Pre-Sell Before Inventory

Method: Create listing → Share with friends → Collect 10 orders → THEN buy inventory
Zero risk: You only buy what you've already sold.

Rule 2: Use Customer Money, Not Yours

Method: Every order pays for itself. Don't use your budget for fulfillment.
Use PayPal/Stripe, hold 30% profit, use rest for reorder.

Rule 3: One Product At A Time

Method: If white thobe works → add navy thobe. If it fails → pivot or stop.
Don't bet on 8 products at once.

Rule 4: Kill Losers Fast

Rule: If ad ROAS <1.5x after 5 days → KILL IT. If product no sales in 14 days → change approach.
Don't throw good money after bad.

Rule 5: Always Keep Cash Reserve

Rule: Never spend below $700 in your bank account.
Even if everything fails, you can return inventory or pivot.
