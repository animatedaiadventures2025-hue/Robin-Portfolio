## 3. Shipping Cost Analysis

### Domestic (India)
- Courier: ₹40-80 per kg (Delhivery, FedEx)
- For 1kg package (3-4 items): ₹80-150
- Bulk shipping (10kg+): ₹25-40/kg via rail/freight

### International (USA)
- **Option 1: India Post (Surface)**
  - 500g-2kg: ₹400-800
  - Time: 20-40 days
  - Good for bulk, not urgent

- **Option 2: DHL/FedEx Express**
  - 500g: ₹450-600
  - 1kg: ₹700-900
  - Time: 3-5 days
  - Best for samples/small orders

- **Option 3: ePacket (China/USPS)**
  - Not available from India directly

### Shipping Strategy for Startup
| Order Value | Shipping Free? | Customer Pays |
|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| < $50 | No | $7.99 flat |
| $50-100 | Yes | Free |
| > $100 | Yes | Free |

**Cost to Customer:** Build in $7.99 flat rate for orders under $50

## 4. Returns & Return Shipping

### Industry Standards
- **30-day return window** (common in USA)
- **Return shipping:** Who pays?

### Options for Startup
| Policy | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| Customer pays return | Lower cost | Lower conversions |
| Store pays return (first 30 days) | Better UX | Higher costs |
| Store pays (defects only) | Fair | Requires QC |

### Recommended Policy
- **30-day returns** for change of mind
- **Customer pays return shipping** ($6-8)
- **Store pays** if defective/wrong item
- **Free return shipping** for orders over $75

### Return Rate Assumption
- Clothing e-commerce: 15-25% return rate
- Islamic modest wear: Possibly lower (10-15%)
- Budget for: 15% returns = factor into pricing

## 5. Startup Considerations Checklist

### Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Register business (LLC in USA recommended)
- [ ] Get Tax ID (EIN)
- [ ] Sales tax nexus (if selling in multiple states)
- [ ] Import compliance (India to USA)
- [ ] Product safety certificates (CPSIA for kids)

### Financial
- [ ] Initial inventory: ₹2-3 lakhs (~$3000-4000)
- [ ] Working capital for repeat orders
- [ ] Buffer for returns (15-20% of revenue)
- [ ] Payment gateway fees (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction)
- [ ] Marketing budget (10-15% of revenue)

### Operations
- [ ] Sourcing from Tirupur (minimum 100 pieces per SKU)
- [ ] Quality control process
- [ ] Warehousing (home or 3PL)
- [ ] Packaging (branded boxes, poly mailers)
- [ ] Shipping partner (India Post vs courier)

### Marketing
- [ ] Instagram presence (target: Muslim moms in USA)
- [ ] Facebook ads targeted by religion/interest
- [ ] Influencer marketing (Islamic parenting accounts)
- [ ] SEO for "kids Islamic clothing", "thobe for boys"
- [ ] Email capture for abandoned carts

### Key Success Factors
1. **Niche focus** - Not competing with large e-commerce
2. **Quality over quantity** - Better fabrics, better margins
3. **Fast shipping** - USA warehouse or quick India-USA delivery
4. **Cultural understanding** - Know the market (Eid, Ramadan)
5. **Return policy** - Build trust, make it easy
