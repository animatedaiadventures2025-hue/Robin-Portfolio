Help Center

Got questions? We've got answers. Browse our FAQs below or contact us directly.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 30 countries. International shipping typically takes 10-15 business days. Customs fees may apply.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also check your order status in your account.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day returns on all unused items in original packaging. Full details here.

How do I start a return?

Email us at returns@nomadgear.com with your order number, and we'll send you a prepaid return label.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Absolutely. Just let us know the size you need and we'll send the replacement as soon as we receive your return.

Products & Warranty

What does the 2-year warranty cover?

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and material failures under normal use. It does not cover damage from misuse or normal wear and tear.

How do I care for my gear?

Each product comes with care instructions. Generally: spot clean, air dry, and store in a cool dry place.
