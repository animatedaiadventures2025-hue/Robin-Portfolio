Backpacks

Backpack sizing is based on torso length, not height:

  • Short: Torso 15-17" (5'0" - 5'5")
  • Regular: Torso 17-19" (5'5" - 5'11")
  • Long: Torso 19-21" (5'11" - 6'3")

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips
  • Torso: From the C7 vertebra (base of neck) to the top of your hip bone
